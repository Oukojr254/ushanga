OUR APPROACH

Promoting The
Beadwork Industry

We encourage our women to take pride in their culture through training them, showcasing and selling their products, passing down the culture to the next generation and preservation of culture for economic and social benefits

Our growth as Ushanga Kenya has been significant as our reach has been seen in 7 counties that practice bead-work namely; Samburu, Kajiado, Narok, West Pokot, Turkana, Baringo, and Marsabit.
