The Ushanga Kenya Initiative is a program created by the Government of Kenya, anchored under the Ministry of Sports, Culture and Heritage.

Ushanga empowers women from pastoral communities by transforming their traditional art into tradable commodities for significant economic and social benefit to the women and their communities
